📚 Linguistic Confluences

This index provides etymological profiles of 40 rivers, lakes, and valley spots. The linguistic distribution reflects:

  • Sanskrit / Vedic: Found in ancient river systems (e.g. Vitasta, Iravati).
  • Persian & Arabic: Introduced during Mughal and Sultanate periods (e.g. Gulmarg, Kausar Nag).
  • Tibetan / Ladakhi: Defining Ladakh desert terrains (e.g. Pangong, Tso Moriri).
  • Dardic / Shina / Yarkandi: Border passes and valleys (e.g. Drass, Gurez, Shyok).
