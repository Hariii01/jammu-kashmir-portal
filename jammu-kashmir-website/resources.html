Legal Texts

Primary Legal Documents

The foundational legal instruments governing J&K's constitutional history.

📜
Instrument of Accession (October 1947)
The legal document signed by Maharaja Hari Singh transferring defence, external affairs, and communications to the Dominion of India. This forms the foundational basis of J&K's relationship with India. Available on Wikisource and archival repositories.
View on Wikipedia ↗
⚖️
Article 370 of the Indian Constitution
Constitutional provision (Part XXI — Temporary, Transitional and Special Provisions) that granted J&K special autonomous status from 1949 until its abrogation in 2019. Full constitutional text available on India Code.
Wikipedia overview ↗
🏛️
Jammu and Kashmir Reorganisation Act, 2019
Parliamentary legislation that bifurcated the former state of J&K into two Union Territories: Jammu & Kashmir (with legislature) and Ladakh (without legislature). Effective 31 October 2019.
India Code — Acts of Parliament ↗
📰
Presidential Orders (1954 & 2019)
The Constitution (Application to J&K) Order, 1954 and the 2019 Presidential Orders that superseded it and made all Constitutional provisions applicable to J&K. Available in the Gazette of India.
Gazette of India ↗
🏛️
Supreme Court Constitution Bench Verdict (11 Dec 2023)
The five-judge Constitution Bench unanimously upheld the constitutionality of the abrogation of Article 370. 23 petitions were disposed. Available on the Supreme Court of India website and major law reporters (SCC, AIR, etc.).

Citizen Services

J&K Citizen’s Handbook & Directory

A practical guide to online applications, administrative powers, civil rights, local laws, and seismic safety for everyday residents.

📝 Applying for a Domicile Certificate Online

A Domicile Certificate is mandatory for local employment, higher education admissions, and welfare schemes. You can apply digitally through the following steps:

  1. Register online: Visit the official e-Unnat Portal or JK e-Services and register as a citizen.
  2. Select service: Log in, go to the services list, and choose "Application for Domicile Certificate."
  3. Fill out the form: Complete the personal and residency details accurately.
  4. Upload documents: If you hold a Permanent Resident Card (PRC), upload copies of your PRC and Aadhaar. If not, upload proof of J&K residency for 15 years (e.g. voter list entry, electricity bills, school certificate).
  5. Track & Download: Submit the application. Once approved (usually within 15 working days), download the digitally signed certificate directly from your dashboard.

⚖️ Division of Administrative Powers: CM vs. LG

Under the J&K Reorganisation Act, administrative control is divided between the elected assembly and the central representative:

Jurisdiction Elected Government (Chief Minister & Cabinet) Lieutenant Governor (LG) & Central Executive
Core Departments Education, Health, Agriculture, Public Works (PWD), Tourism, Forests, Municipalities. Police, Public Order, Land Administration, Civil Services control (transfers/postings).
Legislative Reach Passes laws concerning local development, welfare, and municipal ordinances. Final assent authority on all regional bills; retains control over security matters.

📖 5 Essential Central Laws Empowering Citizens

  • Right to Information (RTI) Act, 2005: Empowers citizens to demand official records and financial transparency details from any government department.
  • Right to Education (RTE) Act, 2009: Guarantees free and compulsory schooling for all children aged 6 to 14 in local government schools.
  • Forest Rights Act, 2006: Recognizes and vests land ownership and forest resource rights in traditional forest-dwelling communities.
  • SC/ST Prevention of Atrocities Act, 1989: Provides legal protections and fast-track courts to safeguard marginalized groups.
  • Consumer Protection Act, 2019: Provides a digital forum to resolve complaints against defective goods or misleading services.

🌾 Land Ownership & Property Tax Regulations

  • Agricultural Land Protection: Only permanent residents of J&K are eligible to purchase agricultural land. Agricultural plots cannot be converted for commercial use without obtaining permission from the District Collector.
  • Property Tax Rules: Property tax is applicable only within urban municipal limits (Srinagar & Jammu). It excludes all agricultural lands, vacant plots, and residential houses with a built-up area of less than 1,000 sq ft.

🚨 Earthquake Preparedness Checklist (Seismic Zones IV & V)

Since J&K lies in high-risk seismic zones, citizens are advised to follow these precautions:

  • ✔ Design Codes: Build houses according to Indian Standard codes for earthquake-resistant structures.
  • ✔ Secure Furniture: Secure tall cupboards, wardrobes, and TV sets to the walls to prevent tip-overs.
  • ✔ Clear Corridors: Keep stairs and exits free from furniture and boxes.
  • ✔ Emergency Pack: Keep a bag with a torch, first-aid kit, whistle, water, dry food, and a folder of essential documents.
  • ✔ Safe spots: Identify safe shelter points in every room, such as under a sturdy wooden table (Drop, Cover, and Hold).
